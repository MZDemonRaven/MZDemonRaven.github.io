About Sheryl Clyde / Thunderbird

I’m Sheryl Clyde, known online as Thunderbird. I’m a DES survivor, gospel rock artist, spiritual warrior, and open-source technologist. My work spans decades in web design, chatbot development, and prophetic music. I create spiritual war hymns and resilience tools for those navigating persecution, trauma, or the coming digital lockdowns.

Through my music and projects, I stand for the remnant — the ones who still burn for Jesus Christ in a world trying to silence us. I don’t sugarcoat truth. I don’t serve platforms. I serve the King.

Mission

To shake the gates of hell with sound. To remind the weary that they’re not alone. To declare that the Lion still roars — and the Bride still burns.
